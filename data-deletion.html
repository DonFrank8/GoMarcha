You have the right to request deletion of your personal data from Marcha at any time. We process all requests within 30 days.

Data We Will Delete
  • Submitted contact information (name and email)
  • Event submissions associated with you
  • Uploaded images associated with your submissions
  • Support communications and correspondence
Data We May Retain
  • Records required to comply with legal obligations
  • Security and fraud-prevention logs
  • Backup copies (auto-expire within 90 days)

Deletion Timeline

30

Days to process

We complete all verified deletion requests within 30 days of receipt, as required by GDPR Art. 12.

90

Days for backups

Backup copies automatically expire and are purged within 90 days, after which your data is irrecoverably removed.

How to Send Your Request

Send an email to the address below. No special form is required — a plain email with your subject line and a brief description is sufficient.

Email support@gomarcha.com Subject line: "Delete My Data"

In your email, please include:

  • The email address you used when submitting events or contacting us
  • A brief description of the data you would like deleted (e.g. a specific event submission, uploaded image, or all data)
No account needed: Marcha does not require user registration. If you submitted an event or uploaded an image, reference the event name and approximate date so we can locate your data accurately.

Your Rights Under GDPR

GDPR Art. 17

Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"). You have the right to request the deletion of personal data we hold about you where it is no longer necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, where you withdraw consent, or where you object to processing and there is no overriding legitimate interest to continue. We will action your request within 30 days unless a legal obligation requires us to retain specific records.

GDPR Art. 15–16

Right of Access and Rectification. You may also request a copy of your personal data (Art. 15) or ask us to correct inaccurate data (Art. 16) by emailing support@gomarcha.com with the subject "GDPR Data Request" or "Correct My Data".

If you are not satisfied with how we handle your request, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish data protection authority: Agencia Española de Protección de Datos (AEPD) at www.aepd.es.
