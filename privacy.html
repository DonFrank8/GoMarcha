Introduction

Welcome to Marcha ("Marcha", "we", "us", or "our"). Marcha is a lifestyle event discovery platform focused on Marbella, the Costa del Sol, and other destinations. Our platform is available at gomarcha.com as a Progressive Web App (PWA) and as native mobile applications for Android and iOS.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

By using Marcha, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of the platform.

This policy applies to all users regardless of where they are located, including users in the European Union, European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and the United States. EU/EEA users receive all rights granted by the GDPR.

Event Submissions

When you submit an event through the "Add Event" form, we collect the following information:

  • Event details: Title, date and time, venue, address, description, genre / category, ticket link, website
  • Submitter details: Your name and contact email address
  • Optional media: Event images (see Section 5)

How Submitted Events Are Used

Event information is reviewed by our team before publication. Once approved, event details are displayed publicly on the Marcha platform and may be shared via our social media channels. Your personal contact details (name and email) are stored for administrative purposes and are not displayed publicly.

Public visibility: Event title, date, venue, description, and images are publicly accessible. Only submit event information you consent to be publicly displayed.

Legal Basis

Processing of event submission data is necessary for the performance of the service you have requested (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR) — namely, listing your event on the Marcha platform. Submitter contact details are processed on the basis of our legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) in being able to follow up regarding your submission.

Uploaded Images

When you upload an event image through the submission form, that image is stored in Supabase Storage (a cloud storage service). Once the associated event is approved and published, the image is publicly accessible via a permanent URL and is displayed alongside the event listing.

Your Responsibilities

By uploading an image, you confirm that:

  • You hold the rights to that image, or have obtained permission from the rights holder.
  • The image does not depict minors without appropriate parental consent.
  • The image does not violate any applicable laws or third-party rights.

Image Deletion

You may request deletion of an uploaded image at any time by contacting us at support@gomarcha.com. Please include the event name and a description of the image. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Note: Images may be cached by Cloudflare's CDN and cached on users' devices after deletion from our origin storage. Full propagation of deletions may take up to 7 days.

Location Data

Marcha offers location-based event discovery to show you events happening near your current position. Location access is strictly optional.

How Location is Used

  • When you tap "Near me" or enable location, your device's browser or OS will request your permission before any location data is shared with us.
  • Location coordinates are used only within your active session to sort and filter events by proximity.
  • We do not store your location coordinates on our servers.
  • We do not track your location history or movement patterns.
  • We do not share location data with advertising networks.

IP-Based Location

Cloudflare may infer a coarse geographic region from your IP address for the purpose of routing your request to the nearest data centre. This is a technical necessity of CDN operation and does not constitute location tracking.

Legal Basis

Precise location processing is based on your explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR), granted via the browser or OS permission dialog. You may revoke this consent at any time by changing your browser or device settings. Revoking consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before revocation.

Your control: You can use Marcha fully without ever granting location permission. Simply browse by city or search for specific venues and events.

Analytics and Cookies

Cookies and Local Storage

Marcha uses browser localStorage and sessionStorage to remember your preferences (such as language selection and the email address you optionally save when submitting events). These are stored on your device and are not transmitted to third parties.

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or cookie-based behavioural analytics.

Service Worker Cache

As a Progressive Web App (PWA), Marcha uses a Service Worker to cache static assets locally on your device. This allows the app to load faster and function offline. No personal data is stored in the Service Worker cache.

Analytics

We may use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-first, cookieless analytics solution. Cloudflare Web Analytics does not use cookies, does not track individual users across sessions, and does not build user profiles. Data collected includes aggregate page views, visitor counts, and technical metrics.

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or other cookie-based analytics platforms.

EU Cookie Law: Since Marcha does not use tracking or advertising cookies, no cookie consent banner is required for our core functionality. Analytical storage is processed on the basis of our legitimate interest in understanding overall platform usage.

Supabase Data Processing

Marcha uses Supabase as its primary data infrastructure for database, authentication services, file storage, and serverless edge functions. Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf under a Data Processing Agreement (DPA).

What is Stored in Supabase

  • Event records submitted by users (including title, date, venue, description)
  • Submitter name and contact email associated with event submissions
  • Uploaded event images (stored in Supabase Storage)
  • Application logs generated by Edge Functions (social posting, queue processing)

Data Location and Transfers

Supabase stores data in Frankfurt, Germany (EU West — eu-west-1) by default. This means your data remains within the European Economic Area (EEA), which complies with GDPR's requirements for data transfers without additional safeguards.

Supabase's infrastructure is provided by Amazon Web Services (AWS). AWS operates as a sub-processor. For more information, see supabase.com/privacy.

Row-Level Security

Supabase's Row-Level Security (RLS) policies are applied to all tables containing user-identifiable information, ensuring that data is only accessible to authorised parties.

Cloudflare Services

Marcha's web infrastructure is protected and accelerated by Cloudflare, Inc. All traffic to gomarcha.com and our APIs passes through Cloudflare's global network.

Services Used

  • CDN (Content Delivery Network): Static assets (HTML, CSS, images) are cached at Cloudflare edge nodes close to your location for faster delivery.
  • DDoS Protection and Web Application Firewall (WAF): Cloudflare automatically inspects incoming requests to protect against malicious traffic. This involves processing IP addresses and request metadata.
  • Cloudflare Web Analytics: Privacy-preserving, cookieless analytics (see Section 7).
  • Workers / Pages: Edge compute may be used to serve the PWA and handle API routing.

Data Processed by Cloudflare

Cloudflare processes IP addresses, HTTP request headers, and user agent strings as part of normal CDN and security operations. This is a legitimate interest processing basis (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) required to deliver the service securely.

Cloudflare is certified under the EU–US Data Privacy Framework and processes data in accordance with Standard Contractual Clauses. For more information, see cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, accidental loss, destruction, or disclosure.

Technical Measures

  • Encryption in transit: All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2 or higher). Cloudflare enforces HTTPS on all connections.
  • Encryption at rest: Data stored in Supabase is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Supabase RLS policies prevent unauthorised database access at the row level.
  • Access control: Administrative access to the database is restricted to authorised personnel via Supabase's role-based access control.
  • DDoS and WAF protection: Cloudflare protects the platform against malicious requests and automated attacks.
  • Environment isolation: Secret keys and API credentials are stored as environment variables and are never committed to source code.

Organisational Measures

  • Access to personal data is limited to personnel who require it to perform their duties.
  • We review our security practices and update them in response to new threats.

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by Article 33 GDPR. Where the breach is likely to result in a high risk to you personally, we will also notify you directly without undue delay (Article 34 GDPR).

Children's Privacy

Marcha is intended for users who are 16 years of age or older. In accordance with Article 8 of the GDPR, we do not knowingly collect or process personal data from children under the age of 16 without verifiable parental consent.

Many events listed on Marcha (live music, nightlife, bars) are intended for adult audiences only. The platform does not verify the age of visitors, but event organisers are responsible for ensuring their events comply with applicable age restrictions.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child under the age of 16 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at support@gomarcha.com. We will investigate and delete the relevant data within 14 days.

For US users (COPPA): Marcha does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are under 13, please do not use this platform.
